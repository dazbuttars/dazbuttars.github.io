Initial Weather Website Planning Document

Site Purpose Statement

The purpose of this weather website is to provide accurate and reliable weather forecasting for the farmers of Franklin Idaho and the surrounding areas. This will eliminate some risk involved in planning when to plant and harvest their crops.


Audience Description

The audience of this site will range in age from about 12 to 80 with varying education levels most will be married the audience is made up of hard working people who only make around $55,260 per year. The audience will be looking for an accurate weather forecast which will have a direct impact on their yearly income. The majority of the content will be accessed after 9:00pm on DSL connections however there may be some important access made during the day on limited mobile data connections so the site needs to be as light as posable.


Persona

He relies on accurate weather to know when to plant his crops without them freezing and knowing when it will be safe to harvest his crops without them getting rained on.
