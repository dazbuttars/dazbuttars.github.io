Visitor Analysis

There demographics would be 20-40 year old males with double digit incomes and at least a high school education living in the united states. The site meets the needs of the visitor by providing informative articles pertaining to men on dress grooming and social skills. The content was organized very well with the most resent articles the top of the page and older article underneath. There is also a search icon at the top left of the page and a categories option at the top left of the screen that would narrow the articles shown based on the category selected. The site also used larger text with double spaced brackets between paragraphs to make reading on a mobile device easier. The site seemed to facilitate mobile users evidence of this is the way that the category options get moved underneath a slide out button when on the mobile site versed the desktop site.
